Shaik Ismail Shah Qadri

Family Tree & Lineage · Krovvidi, Andhra Pradesh

Shaik Ismail Shah Qadri came from Baghdad together with his four brothers to spread the tableegh of Deen-e-Islam. The ruler of the time sent him to South India, and so he came to settle in Krovvidi, Andhra Pradesh, where his dargah and a mosque stand to this day.

Mosque founded 1524 A.D. · His mazaar lies on the north side of the mosque · Passed away 1001 Hijri / 1592 A.D., during the reign of Quli Qutub Shah of the Deccan.
